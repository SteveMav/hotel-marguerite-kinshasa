# Hotel Marguerite de Kinshasa Prospecting Kit

## Register
brand

## Purpose
Create a polished freelance prospecting demo for Hotel Marguerite de Kinshasa. The site, PDF, and outreach emails should help the hotel imagine a clearer official web presence: easier booking, more trust, cleaner local visibility, and a stronger first impression on mobile.

## Audience
- Hotel decision maker or manager receiving the WhatsApp/PDF proposal.
- Travelers and local visitors who would use the future official site.
- Business guests and families looking for a practical central hotel in Kinshasa.

## Tone
Respectful, concrete, calm, and commercially useful. Avoid claiming the hotel approved or owns the demo. Avoid heavy technical terms. Speak about clarity, trust, direct contact, and a better first impression.

## Known Facts
- Public names include Hotel Marguerite de Kinshasa, MARGUERITE HOTEL, and Hotel Marguerite Officiel.
- The hotel is publicly described as located in Kinshasa city center.
- Club Privileges lists Avenue Kabinda N°62, C/Kinshasa.
- Other directory/map listings mention Avenue de Kabinda or Ave De Kapanga, so the final official address should be confirmed with the hotel.
- Public phone/WhatsApp contact: +243 998 386 650.
- Facebook/directories show an existing local presence with several thousand page likes/check-ins.

## Product Principles
- Treat WhatsApp as the primary conversion path.
- Make every CTA practical and visible on mobile.
- Do not invent prices or unconfirmed amenities.
- Use a tasteful hotel identity that feels local and warm, not generic luxury.
