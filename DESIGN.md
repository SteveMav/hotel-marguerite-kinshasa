# Design Direction

## Creative Sentence
A hotel manager opens the demo on a phone in Kinshasa after receiving a WhatsApp PDF, in daylight, with only one minute to decide whether the proposal feels serious.

## Palette
- Warm ivory: `#f7f1e6`
- Linen: `#efe3d1`
- Deep palm green: `#164b3a`
- Soft sage: `#7d947a`
- Muted burgundy: `#7b2d3a`
- Brass: `#b98a52`
- Charcoal ink: `#20231f`

The palette must not become beige-only. Green carries trust and location, burgundy adds hotel warmth, brass is used sparingly for emphasis.

## Typography
- Display: Bodoni Moda, Georgia fallback.
- Body: Manrope, Segoe UI fallback.
- Letter spacing stays at 0.
- Body lines stay under 75 characters where possible.

## Layout
- Brand register, editorial and hospitality-oriented.
- Asymmetric hero on desktop, single-column and compact on mobile.
- Full-width bands with constrained inner content.
- Cards only for repeated rooms, service items, or contact options.
- No nested cards.

## Motion
- Short CSS transitions on transform, opacity, color, and background.
- Buttons use a subtle press state.
- Reduced motion removes movement while preserving clarity.

## Imagery
The included images are local proposal mood assets, not official hotel photos. They should communicate a warm city hotel direction while the final real project would replace them with verified hotel photography.
