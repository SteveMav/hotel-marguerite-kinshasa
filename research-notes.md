## Notes And Uncertainty
- Address variants differ across directories: Avenue Kabinda N°62, Avenue de Kabinda, and 2068 Ave De Kapanga. The demo uses `Avenue Kabinda N°62, Commune de Kinshasa` as a practical placeholder and explicitly says the address should be confirmed before publication.
- No reliable public source confirmed room categories, prices, restaurant, pool, spa, or meeting facilities. The website presents room/service categories as proposal placeholders to validate with the hotel.

## Prospecting Angle
The hotel already has public discovery signals, but the information is scattered across Facebook, maps, and directories. A clean official website could make booking easier from WhatsApp, centralize the address and phone number, show verified photos, and reduce hesitation for guests comparing central Kinshasa hotels.
